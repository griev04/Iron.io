Iron.io

Instructions

You are a small cell in a wild petri dish filled with fearless rival cells.
Survival is your objective.
Hunt cells smaller than you but flee from bigger ones or you will become an easy snack.
You may also try to bait opponent cells by ejecting part of your mass but beware of traps: you will loose all your precious mass in a blink of an eye.
Good luck, you will need it!

Commands

  • Move mouse to move player (no click needed)
  • Mouse wheel (or keys 1 and 2) for zoom in and zoom out (key 3 resets to default zoom)
  • Space bar: eject mass
  • Esc: pause the game
  • U: hide/unhide user interface
  • T: toggle light and night theme

PAUSE

Press Esc to resume game
